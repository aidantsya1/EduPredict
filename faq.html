Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is EduPredict?

EduPredict is a platform that uses AI to predict student performance based on attendance, internal test scores, and trial exams.

How do I upload student data?

On the Prediction page, educators can upload a CSV file containing student scores and attendance to allow the system to run predictions.

Is student data secure?

Yes, all data is securely stored and follows institutional privacy and data protection standards.

Can students view their predictions?

Yes, students can log in and view their prediction results and simulate their performance using the Simulation feature.

Who can access the system?

All user roles — Admin, Educator, and Student — have access to the system, with different permissions based on their role.
